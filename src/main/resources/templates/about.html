The Goal

"To allow users to locate, reserve, and rent out parking spaces."

Overview

This site connects hosts who want to rent out their unused space with guests seeking to station vehicles safely at affordable "prices". Guests are able to find the right listing using a variety of filters and a booking system allows them to reserve a parking space through any available dates.
