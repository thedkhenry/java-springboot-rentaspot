• How do I contact my host?

    If you've messaged them before, then simply go to your "Messages" page and your host should appear in your contacts list.

    If it's your first time contacting them, then head over to your reservations page, click on your reservation, click on the "Message Host" button, type your message in the pop-up, and send!

  • How long does it take for a reservation to get approved?

    Hosts have one hour to approve or deny reservations. If hosts don't respond within the hour, then the reservation expires and the dates become available to reserve again.

  • How do I review a parking spot?

    In your reservations page, click on the yellow "Review" button on the reservation you'd like to review, then just choose the rating and write a few words about the parking spot. However, you can only review a parking spot on or after the last day of your reservation.

  • How do I change my listing's address?

    Parking spot addresses cannot be changed. You will need to create a new listing if you want to change an address.

  • How can I add a parking spot to my wishlist?

    Simply go to a parking spot's details page and click on the heart icon on top right. All wishlist spots can be viewed in your "Wishlist" page.

  • Do I have to apply to become a host?

    Nope. If you want to list your own parking spot, then just sign up, head over to your "Listings" page, and click on the "Create" button. Fill out the form and publish!
